**Evaluation Date:** November 11, 2025

**Strategy Under Review:** Programmatic SEO Pages for Stock Tickers
**Evaluator:** Product Management Assessment

---

## Executive Summary

### Overall Score: 7.5/10 - STRONG RECOMMEND WITH MODIFICATIONS

### Elevator Pitch
Daily Ticker is building thousands of SEO-optimized stock ticker pages that showcase historical pick performance to convert Google searchers into newsletter subscribers at near-zero CAC.

### Problem Statement  
Potential investors searching for specific stock information (e.g., "NVDA stock newsletter") currently land on generic competitor pages that gate all content. They need to see proof of value before subscribing to another newsletter.

### Key Findings

**STRENGTHS:**
- Excellent product-market fit (addresses real user need)
- Strong competitive differentiation (transparent performance data)
- Scalable implementation (programmatic page generation)
- Low customer acquisition cost (organic SEO)
- Clear conversion path (proof → trust → subscribe)

**WEAKNESSES:**
- Missing critical conversion features (social proof, Pro visibility)
- Legal/compliance risks not fully mitigated (performance claims)
- Tracking infrastructure gaps (can't measure ROI per ticker)
- Monetization strategy underdeveloped (no Pro upsell on pages)

### Recommendation
LAUNCH with modifications. Complete P0 features (legal disclaimers, social proof, sitemap integration) before promoting. Expected 12-month return: $11K-$30K MRR from organic traffic alone.

## 6. Risks & Mitigation: 7/10 - Manageable with Action Required

### CRITICAL RISKS (Must Address Before Scale)

**1. Legal/Compliance - HIGH RISK**

**Risk:** Showing win rates/returns could be considered investment advice without proper disclaimers.

**Mitigation Required:**
- Add disclaimer to EVERY ticker page (not just footer)
- Include "Past performance ≠ future results" near win rate  
- Add "For educational purposes, not financial advice" near metrics
- STRONGLY RECOMMEND legal review of page templates
- Ensure SEC Rule 206(4)-1 compliance

**Example Placement:**
```
[Above Track Record Section]
⚠ DISCLAIMER: Past performance does not guarantee future results.
Daily Ticker is for educational purposes only and is not financial advice.
```

**2. Data Accuracy - MEDIUM RISK**

**Risk:** Incorrect win rates or returns damage trust and create legal exposure.

**Mitigation Required:**
- Automated data validation tests  
- Manual spot-checks of high-traffic pages
- User reporting mechanism ("Report incorrect data")
- Clear methodology explanation ("How we calculate returns")

**3. Stale Content - HIGH RISK**

**Risk:** Ticker pages with no new picks become outdated, hurt SEO.

**Mitigation Required:**
- Add "Last updated" timestamp
- Show real-time market data (keeps fresh)
- Noindex tickers with <3 picks or >6 months stale
- Prioritize high-volume tickers first

### Medium Risks

4. **Duplicate Content** - Risk of thin pages flagged by Google
5. **Poor Performance Handling** - Displaying losses prominently may deter signups
6. **Competitor Scrutiny** - Good ideas get copied
